21 November 2013 .:: BRD donates diary cattle to needy handicapped children of KOMERA

GIRA ICUMBI
ACCOUNT

BRD Gira Icumbi Account is a convenient saving scheme that facilitates any client who wishes to own a house depending on the savings on his/her monthly revenue.

ADVANTAGES OF GIRA ICUMBI ACCOUNT
Your savings earn a competitive annual interest rate of 8% and proceeds deposited through your account. No maintenance fee.
Gira Icumbi Account helps you to access a mortgage loan at discounted interest rate compared to the normal mortgage interest rate of 16%.
