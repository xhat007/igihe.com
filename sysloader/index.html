Bimwe mu byaranze uyu munsi mu mateka

1792: Claude Joseph Rouget de Lisle yahimbye indirimbo yubahiriza igihugu cy’Ubufaransa izwi ku izina rya “La Marseillaise”.

Uyu Claude yavutse tariki 10 Gicurasi 1760 yitaba Imana tariki 26 Kamena 1836, yari umusirikare w’igihugu cy’Ubufaransa wari ufite ipeti rya Kapiteni, yamenyekanye cyane kubera guhimba indirimbo zahaga morali abasirikare, cyane iyaje kumenyekana ni iyo yise La Marsellaise yaje nyuma no guhinduka indirimbo yubahiriza igihugu cy’Ubufaransa.

1901: New York yabaye Leta ya mbere mu zigize Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, mu kwaka impushya zo gutwara ibinyabiziga.

1945: Umunsi ufatwa nk’ukwibohora kw’igihugu cy’Ubutariyan, ubwo Abanazi bavaga mu Majyaruguru y’iki gihugu mu ntambara ya kabiri y’isi yose, icyo gihe uwayoboraga Ubutaliyani Benito Mussolini yahisemo guhunga.

1945: Ibihugu 50 byahuriye mu Mujyi wa San Francisco, muri Leta ya Calfornia mu nama mpuzamahanga yo gutangiza Umuryango w’Abibumye.

1945: Ingabo za nyuma z’igihugu cy’Ubudage zavuye mu gihugu cya Finland, ahitwa Lapland, dore ko n’iyi ntambara yahabereye yaje kuhitirirwa ikaba intambara ya Lapland.

2005: Igihugu cya Bulgaria na Romaniya byashyize umukono ku masezerano yo kwinjira mu Muryango w’ubumwe bw’ibihugu by’I Burayi.

2005: Abantu 107 baguye mu mpanuka ya gari ya moshi yabereye mu gihugu cy’Ubuyapani.

1948: Yu Shyi-kun wabaye Minisitiri w’Intebe wa Taiwan.

1987: Umukinnyi wa Rugby, Johann Smith.

2003: Samson Kitur, Umunyakenya wakinaga imikino ngororangingo.
