Irungu n’ibitotsi mu birori by’imideli ya Mama Africa byabaye kuri uyu wambere (Amafoto)

Yanditswe na Jean Baptiste Nshimiyimana na Munyengabe Murungi Sabin, IGIHE
Kuya 16-05-2016 saa 12:06
Yasuwe : 27885
Yavuzweho : 47
47 2

Mu Mujyi wa Kigali habereye ibirori by’imideli yaranzwe n’umukobwa witwa Niragire Nince [Mama Africa], byitabiriwe n’abantu bagera kuri 20 nabo barushijwe ingufu n’irungu barasinzira abandi bataha bitarangiye kubera uburyo byamaze amasaha menshi.

Ibirori byo kwerekana imideli yiswe Mama Africa Design Season 1 byateguwe na Niragire Nince uyobora Mama Africa izina benshi bamwitiriye. Ni ku nshuro ya mbere bibereye i Kigali herekanwa imyenda yiganjemo iy’Abanyafurika idoze mu bitenge.

Ibirori byabereye kuri Top Tower Hotel[mu igorofa rya munani] ku Kacyiru, mu ijoro ryo kuri uyu wa 15 Gicurasi 2016.

Byagombaga gutangira saa moya zuzuye, kwinjira byasabaga kwishyura amafaranga ibihumbi bitanu ariko icyagaragaye ni uko abacuruzaga amatike basa n’abatashye badakoze ku ifaranga kuko na bake binjiye berekanaga impapuro z’ubutumire (invitation) abandi bari biganjemo abanyamakuru n’abanyamideli ubwabo.

Ucyinjira muri Top Tower Hotel umusekirite yahitaga akubwira ko ascenceur yagize ikibazo bityo ko ugomba kuzamuka mu igorofa rya munani n’amaguru, ibintu bitamenyerewe na benshi. Abazamukaga bageragayo bahagira abandi ukumva bitotomba bavuga ko batabyishimiye.

Ahagana saa tatu n’igice z’ijoro nibwo ibirori byatangiye, habanje kuririmba abahanzi bafite amazina ataramenyekana i Kigali nyuma abasore n’inkumi batangira kwiyerekana.

Muri ibi birori hari irungu rikomeye cyane ahanini kubera uburyo byatwaye amasaha menshi hakiyongeraho ko abantu bari bake, bicaye batatanye ku buryo bitari byoroshye kubona abantu baganira. Kwiyerekana nabyo byagiye bitinda cyane ku buryo hagati y’icyiciro n’ikindi habagamo icyuho kugeza ubwo uwari uyoboye ibirori na we w’umuhanzi mushya yashyizemo CD ye akababyinira ashaka kubamara irungu.

Byabaye iby’ubusa kuko ahagana saa tanu benshi [muri bake baje] bikubuye barataha salle isigaramo abantu batarenze 15 nabwo biganjemo abanyamakuru. Mu gihe cyose ibi birori byatindaga, abafana wasangaga baryamye mu ntebe, abafite za smartphone bakimarira irungu kuri WhatsApp na Facebook.

Ibirori byasojwe ahagana saa tanu n’igice z’ijoro, Niragire Nince yavuze ko yishimiye uko byagenze ndetse ngo yizeye ko icyiciro cya kabiri ateganya gukora vuba kizagenda neza.
