RWANDA DISPATCH
About Us
"Rwanda Dispatch" is a monthly news magazine that covers news analysis, informed commentaries and researched reports on Rwanda's social, economic and political issues.

The magazine is accredited by the Media High Council as an English News Magazine, interpreted into a Kinyarwanda Version in a Bi-monthly Newspaper called Isonga, circulating in Rwanda, EAC member countries and beyond.
